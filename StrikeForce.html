Strike Force – Urban War in Nova Aeternum

Tactical combat in virtual reality inside a futuristic metropolis.

Explore Nova Aeternum, a capital city on a technologically advanced planet.

In this next-generation VR shooter,

two teams face off in the midst of full-scale urban warfare,

while simultaneously battling a common

enemy: a highly trained paramilitary army. Intense action, strategic cooperation, and stunning realism in a relentless multiplayer experience.

Project Developed by UVR

At UVR, we brought this virtual reality experience to life from the ground up. Our multidisciplinary team worked across every stage of development to deliver a high-quality, immersive, and visually striking result.

What did we do in this project?

The UVR team was responsible for every aspect of production, both artistic and technical:

-3D Modeling: We designed all environments, characters, and game assets with a strong focus on detail and VR optimization.

-Rigging: We implemented bone structures for precise and efficient animation of characters and interactive objects.

-Texturing & UV Mapping: We created realistic and optimized textures, carefully managing every UV map.

-Animation: We brought characters, enemies, and interactive elements to life to enhance player immersion.

-Story & Scriptwriting: We crafted an engaging narrative to accompany the player throughout the experience.

-Video & Sound Editing: We produced audiovisual elements for trailers and cinematics with immersive sound design.

-Programming: We developed the full game logic, interaction systems, AI, and gameplay mechanics for VR.

-Visual Effects (VFX): We added futuristic effects that reinforce the game’s aesthetic and the atmosphere of its universe.

Thanks to a powerful mix of creativity, technology, and futuristic vision, this project showcases UVR's capability to create next-generation video games and virtual reality experiences.
